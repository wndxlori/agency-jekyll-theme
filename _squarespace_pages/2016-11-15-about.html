About Lori

Founder of The WNDX Group

A software consultancy that specializes in Ruby on Rails, iOS/Mac Development, and Software Training.

Got my start in software straight out of school, a B.Sc. in Computer Science from University of Calgary, working in the Calgary oil patch.  Since then, I've developed software for the health, finance, and entertainment industries, as well as products used by other software developers.

When I'm not developing applications, I enjoying speaking at meetups and conferences, both local and abroad.  I also enjoy teaching, and currently offer 2 Ruby on Rails based training workshops.

Passions

I am passionate about software craftsmanship, and the application of continuous improvement to software, software developers, and software development teams.

I'm also a card-carrying member of DevChix, and a teacher at RailsBridge, and a mentor and instructor for Ladies Learning Code.  Helping to make a career in software development into a more welcoming opportunity for all women is very, very important to me.

When I'm not working, you'll find me spending time with my husband and my dogs.
